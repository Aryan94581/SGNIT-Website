FAQs & Help for SGNIT

  1. Home
  2. About us
  3. FAQs

1. What courses do you offer?

We offer a variety of courses, including Web Development, Mobile App Development, Data Science, Machine Learning, and more.

2. Are the courses online or offline?

Our courses are offered both online and offline, allowing students to choose their preferred learning mode.

3. What is the duration of your courses?

The course duration ranges from 3 months to 1 year, depending on the program you choose.

4. Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide placement assistance to our students through resume building, interview preparation, and job referrals.

5. What is the fee structure?

The fees vary depending on the course. You can contact us for detailed fee information or visit the respective course pages.

6. What are the prerequisites for joining your courses?

Most of our courses are beginner-friendly and do not require prior knowledge. Advanced courses may have specific prerequisites listed on their pages.

7. Do you provide certifications?

Yes, all our courses include a certificate of completion after successful completion of the program.

8. How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by visiting our website, selecting the desired course, and completing the registration process online.

9. Can I get a refund if I am not satisfied?

Yes, we have a refund policy in place. You can request a refund within the first week of the course if you are not satisfied.

10. What is the batch size for your classes?

We maintain a small batch size of 10-15 students to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.

11. Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials, including notes, video lectures, and project guides for all our courses.

12. Are there any discounts available?

We offer discounts for early bird registrations and group enrollments. Check our website for current offers.

13. What kind of support do you provide for students?

We offer 24/7 technical support, doubt-clearing sessions, and mentorship throughout the course duration.

14. Do you provide hands-on project experience?

Yes, all our courses include hands-on projects and assignments to help students apply their learning practically.

15. Can I contact the instructor for queries?

Yes, students can directly contact instructors during live sessions or through our discussion forums.
